Company Overview

Creative India House is a new established company that make hand-made and creative products with the creative and hardworking team from village.

We improve the skills of rural community and monetise their skills and creativities by giving them opportunity to work with us. With Creative India House the village girls can stand by their own and become self independent.

Creative India House sells the hand-made sustainable and attractive products through its own website at low price. The customer satisfaction is our priority as we believe on our products quality and production team. With our best hand-made products we will welcome the feedbacks and suggestions from our customers to improve the product quality.

    Business Strategy

    Mission

    Our mission is to provide job with the best environment to the village girls so that they can earn with their skills and become self independent.
    Our mission is to provide the job in the village areas because we see the most girls in the villages who are educated or skilled but have no job so that they can earn or show their skills.

    Vision

    While we are starting from a small level but as we will improve our market places with our product quality, our vision is to provide the huge number of jobs to the village girls who are eager to stand by their own and want to become self independent.
    To give the an environment in which the our team can improve their skills and they can choose the work field as per their skills and interests.

    Goals

    Our goal is to help the girls from village areas to improve their talent and skills in the field they are interested. We see the girls are very talented either in making any handcraft, hand-made product, singing, dancing, drawing, art, decorating their home, cooking, or painting.
    They have the problem solving mind but without any platform or chances their talent and skills are worthless. And this is major factor their family marry them soon without their future or career.

    Values

    Its just an initiative to empower the girls from village areas (rural community).

    Selection of Team

    The selection of the candidates will be based on their skills and interest in different fields.
    They can choose their interesting tasks in which they can give their best and improve their skills and knowledge day by day.
